Salesforce IoT Cloud

From November 2014 - November 2015, I was fortuate enough to contribute part-time in 3 different roles on the Salesforce IoT Cloud team (which was launched at Dreamforce 2015): Software Development, UI Design, and UX Research.

It has been an amazing learning experience for me, to have been shaping the product from the earliest days until launch at Dreamforce 2015, and work with such a great team as it grew from around 25 to 85 within just several months. I will update this page soon with more details about my internship.
