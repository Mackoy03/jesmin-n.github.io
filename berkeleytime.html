Berkeleytime

As part of the Associated Students of the University of California, I work in the Office of the Chief TechnologyOfficer as a Staff Engineer. Our team of ~22 are building a comprehensive tool for students to:
  • Search through 8000+ courses from the UC Berkeley catalog, and apply filters for requirements and majors
  • See customized graphs to compare historic grade distributions and enrollment history to help you make the best course decisions
  • View a map of campus along with all the classes happening in each building, so you can find an empty room or interesting lecture to attend
    • Specifically, I am working to redesign the search experience as well as building a dashboard. You can see the product here.
